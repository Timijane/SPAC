Our Story & Vision

Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church

A Legacy of Faith & Transformation

Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC) is more than just a place of worship; it is a dynamic spiritual family committed to proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ with power and authenticity. Founded on biblical principles and guided by the Holy Spirit, SPAC stands as a beacon of hope, healing, and transformation in our community and beyond.

Our church is built on the foundation of God's Word, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and driven by a passion to see lives radically changed through the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ. We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit in the lives of believers.

At SPAC, we embrace diversity and welcome people from all walks of life to experience genuine fellowship, grow in spiritual maturity, and discover their God-given purpose. Our community is characterized by authentic worship, sound biblical teaching, compassionate service, and a deep commitment to making disciples who will impact their world for Christ.

Our Founder

Prophet Samuel Adegboyega

Founder & Visionary Leader of SPAC

"God has called us not just to gather in buildings, but to build His kingdom in the hearts of people. Our mandate is to raise champions who will proclaim salvation with power and transform nations through the anointing of the Holy Spirit."

Prophet Samuel Adegboyega is a man of profound spiritual insight, unwavering faith, and deep compassion. Under his visionary leadership, SPAC has grown from a small gathering of believers to a vibrant, multinational ministry impacting thousands of lives. His ministry is characterized by accurate prophetic utterances, deliverance, healing, and a relentless passion for soul-winning.

With over three decades of ministerial experience, Prophet Adegboyega continues to inspire believers to pursue intimacy with God, embrace their spiritual gifts, and fulfill their divine destinies. His teachings emphasize practical Christianity, Holy Spirit empowerment, and the manifestation of God's kingdom on earth.

Our Core Identity

Our Vision

To raise a global community of Spirit-filled believers who manifest God's kingdom on earth through powerful proclamation, authentic worship, and transformative service.

We envision a church where every member operates in their God-given gifts, where families are restored, communities are transformed, and nations are impacted by the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To proclaim the gospel of salvation with apostolic authority, making disciples of all nations through sound teaching, Holy Spirit empowerment, and compassionate ministry.

  • To teach believers to live victoriously through God's Word
  • To equip saints for ministry through spiritual gifts
  • To provide a nurturing environment for spiritual growth
  • To demonstrate God's love through community outreach
  • To raise leaders who will transform their spheres of influence

Our Core Tenets

We believe in the fundamental truths of Christianity as revealed in the Bible:

  • The divine inspiration and authority of Scripture
  • The Trinity: God the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • The virgin birth, and resurrection of Jesus Christ
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Christ alone
  • The present ministry of the Holy Spirit and spiritual gifts
  • The imminent return of Jesus Christ and eternal life
  • The priesthood of all believers and the Great Commission

Our Journey Through Time

1995

The Birth of a Vision

SPAC was founded by Prophet Samuel Adegboyega with a small group of believers in Lagos, Nigeria. The initial meetings were characterized by intense prayer, prophetic ministry, and a burning passion to proclaim salvation.

2002

Establishing Our Foundation

The church acquired its first permanent worship center, marking a significant milestone in our growth. Ministries were established, and the church began to experience notable increase in membership and spiritual impact.

2010

Expansion & International Outreach

SPAC launched its first international branch in the United Kingdom, followed by branches in the United States. This period marked our transition from a local church to a global ministry with a multinational footprint.

2018

Digital Transformation

We embraced technology to reach a global audience through online services, streaming platforms, and digital discipleship programs. Our virtual campus became a vital extension of our physical ministry.

Present

A Growing Global Family

Today, SPAC ministers to thousands of believers across multiple continents through our campuses, online platforms, and various ministry arms. We continue to innovate while remaining steadfast to our founding vision and values.
