Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about joining SPAC Church

Is there a membership fee to join? +
No, there is no membership fee to join SPAC Church. We believe church membership should be accessible to everyone. However, we encourage all members to participate in tithing and offering as an act of worship and to support the ministry.
How long does the membership process take? +
The complete membership process typically takes 4-6 weeks. This includes attending services, completing the new members class (4 weeks), and meeting with a pastor. We want to ensure you have a good understanding of our church before making a commitment.
Can I transfer membership from another church? +
Yes, we accept membership transfers from other Bible-believing churches. You'll still need to complete our new members class to understand SPAC Church's specific vision and values, but we'll acknowledge your previous church membership.
What if I'm not sure about committing yet? +
That's perfectly fine! You're welcome to attend our services and participate in activities without becoming a member. Many people attend for several months before deciding to join. Take your time to get to know our church family.
