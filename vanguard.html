The Zealous Generation

Army of Firebrands

Vanguard is not just a youth group - we are an army of firebrands, ignited by the Holy Spirit and commissioned to take the gospel to the streets, campuses, and nations. We are young believers filled with passion, courage, and uncompromising faith.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to reach every young person with the life-changing message of Jesus Christ. We believe that youth are not just the church of tomorrow but powerful agents of transformation today.

At Vanguard, we combine deep spiritual formation with radical evangelism. We train, equip, and release young people to operate in the power of the Holy Spirit, demonstrating God's love through both word and deed.

Discover Our Values

Our Core Pillars

Holy Fire

We are baptized with the Holy Spirit and fire. Our meetings are characterized by passionate worship, deep intercession, and manifestations of God's power. We believe in the present-day operation of spiritual gifts.

Bold Proclamation

We take the gospel beyond the four walls of the church. Through street evangelism, campus outreach, and digital missions, we boldly declare the good news of Jesus Christ without compromise or fear.

Community Impact

Our faith is demonstrated through action. We engage in community service, humanitarian projects, and social justice initiatives that reflect God's heart for the marginalized and broken.

Discipleship & Mentoring

We invest in raising the next generation of leaders. Through small groups, mentorship programs, and leadership training, we equip young believers to fulfill their God-given destiny.

Our Fire Activities

Street Evangelism

Weekly outreach programs where we hit the streets with the gospel, prophetic ministry, and healing prayers. We see salvations, deliverances, and miracles in marketplaces, campuses, and public squares.

Every Saturday 3:00 PM

Fire Night Vigils

Monthly all-night prayer meetings filled with intense worship, prophetic declarations, and intercession for revival. These nights are marked by supernatural encounters and spiritual breakthroughs.

Last Friday Monthly 10:00 PM - 5:00 AM

Youth Power Rallies

Quarterly massive gatherings of youth from across the region featuring dynamic preaching, worship concerts, and miracle services. Thousands of young people gather for these life-changing events.

Quarterly 5000+ Expected

Firebrand Discipleship

Weekly training sessions covering topics like prayer, evangelism, spiritual warfare, leadership, and discovering your spiritual gifts. Equipping believers for effective ministry.

Every Tuesday 6:00 PM

Mission Trips

Domestic and international mission trips to unreached communities. We partner with local churches to conduct crusades, medical missions, and community development projects.

Bi-Annually Various Locations

Creative Arts Ministry

Using drama, music, dance, poetry, and visual arts to communicate the gospel in culturally relevant ways. Our creative team produces powerful presentations for outreach events.

Weekly Rehearsals Multiple Teams
