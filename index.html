We are SPAC

Welcome to SPAC Church

SPAC Church is a vibrant, multicultural community of believers committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples of all nations. Founded on the principles of faith, love, and hope, we are dedicated to worship, fellowship, and service.

Our mission is to raise champions and nurture leaders who will transform their world through the power of the Holy Spirit. Through dynamic worship, sound biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach, we strive to impact our community and beyond.

Join us as we worship, learn, and serve together in unity and love, building a church that reflects God's kingdom here on earth.
