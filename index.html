Corporate Social Responsibility

Making a positive difference in the society

The Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) was formed over 10 years ago as a community development arm of SPAC Church, to impact our local community and give back to society. Our focus is on 5 core areas:

Skills Development

Through education, enterprise and employability training

Food Bank & Poverty Alleviation

Providing food support to those in need

Tackling Homelessness

Assisting the homeless with shelter & resources

Health & Environment

Improving health, safety, and environmental standards

Collaboration & Partnership

Working with communities and organizations for positive change
