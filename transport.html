Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our transport services answered.

Is the transport service really free?
Yes, absolutely free! SPAC Church provides transportation as a ministry service to ensure everyone can attend worship regardless of their location or transportation challenges. There are no hidden charges or fees.
How early should I register for transport?
We recommend registering at least 24 hours before the service you plan to attend. This helps us allocate adequate vehicles and seats. However, we do accept last-minute registrations subject to availability.
What if I miss the pickup?
If you miss your scheduled pickup, please contact our transport coordinator immediately at 0800-SPAC-BUS. We'll do our best to arrange alternative transportation or advise on the next available pickup.
Can I bring children on the shuttle?
Yes, children are welcome! Please indicate the number of children when registering so we can provide appropriate seating. Child safety seats are available upon request with advance notice.
What happens after the service?
Return trips depart from the church 15 minutes after each service ends. Buses return to the same pickup points. The return schedule is announced at the end of each service.
