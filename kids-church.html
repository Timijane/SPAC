WELCOME TO KIDS CHURCH!

A place where children learn about Jesus in a way they can understand and enjoy.

Your Child's Spiritual Home

At SPAC Kids Church, we believe children are not just the future of the church—they're vital members today! Our dedicated team creates engaging, age-appropriate environments where kids can build a strong foundation of faith while having fun.

Through interactive Bible lessons, worship, games, and creative activities, we help children develop a personal relationship with Jesus that will guide them throughout their lives.

  • Age-appropriate Bible teaching
  • Safe, secure check-in system
  • Trained, background-checked volunteers
  • Creative worship and activities
  • Parent resources and support

PROGRAMS BY AGE

Each age group has specially designed activities and lessons.

Little Lambs

Ages 0-2 Years

Our nursery provides a safe, nurturing environment for our youngest attendees. While parents attend service, babies experience God's love through caring attention, simple Bible stories, and age-appropriate play.

  • Loving, consistent caregivers
  • Simple worship songs
  • Secure check-in/out system
  • Available all service times

Explorers

Ages 3-5 Years

Preschoolers discover God's love through interactive Bible stories, creative play, music, and hands-on activities. We focus on foundational truths: God made me, God loves me, and Jesus wants to be my friend.

  • Interactive Bible stories
  • Creative crafts
  • Play-based learning
  • Small group activities

Adventurers

Ages 6-8 Years

Elementary kids engage with God's Word through dynamic lessons, worship, games, and small group discussions. We help them understand how Bible truths apply to their everyday lives.

  • Engaging Bible lessons
  • Kid-friendly worship
  • Interactive games
  • Small group discussions

Trailblazers

Ages 9-12 Years

Preteens dive deeper into Scripture and learn to apply biblical principles to real-life situations. We prepare them for the teen years with solid teaching and opportunities to serve.

  • Bible study skills
  • Service opportunities
  • Peer relationships
  • Q&A discussions

YOUR CHILD'S SAFETY

We take safety seriously so you can worship with peace of mind.

Secure Check-In

Every child receives a unique security tag. Parents get a matching tag for pickup.

Trained Volunteers

All team members complete background checks and child safety training.

Health & Wellness

Clean rooms, sanitized toys, and first-aid trained staff in every area.

Monitored Areas

All children's areas have security cameras and open-door policies.
