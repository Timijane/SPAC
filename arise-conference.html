Each first Saturday of the month, we gather not just for another church program, but for a divine appointment—a time where heaven meets earth in the lives of young people.

This is where destinies are clarified, spiritual gifts are activated, and a generation arises to take its place in God's kingdom agenda.

MONTHLY GATHERING SCHEDULE

A typical ARISE Conference flow. Times may vary as we follow the Spirit's leading.

6:30 PM - Arrival & Fellowship

Welcome & Connection

Arrive early for pre-service fellowship, prayer, and connecting with other young believers.

7:00 PM - Opening Session

Worship & Praise

High-energy worship led by the ARISE worship team. Creating an atmosphere for encounter.

MEET OUR HOST

The visionary behind the ARISE Conference movement.

Visionary & Host

Pastor Yomi Okewunmi

A dynamic minister with over 15 years of experience in youth and young adult ministry, Pastor Yomi carries a unique apostolic and prophetic mantle for awakening a generation. His passion is to see young people encounter God in transformative ways and step into their divine assignments.

With a background in theology and practical ministry, Pastor Yomi combines deep biblical insight with relatable application for today's youth. He serves as the Youth and Young Adult Pastor at SPAC Church and travels nationally speaking at youth conferences and leadership summits.

"My assignment is to sound the alarm for this generation to ARISE from slumber and take their place in God's end-time agenda."
