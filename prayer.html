Prayer

Then they cried to the LORD in their trouble, and he delivered them from their distress. He made the storm be still, and the waves of the sea were hushed. Then they were glad that the waters were quiet, and he brought them to their desired haven. Psalm 107:28-30

At SPAC Church, we believe in the power of prayer. Prayer is our direct line of communication with God, and through it, we find strength, guidance, healing, and miracles.

Our prayer ministry is available to support you in your spiritual journey. Whether you need prayer for healing, deliverance, breakthrough, or guidance, our prayer team is ready to stand with you in agreement.

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God."

Philippians 4:6
