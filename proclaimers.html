The Stage of Redemption

Drama With Divine Purpose

Proclaimers Drama Ministry is more than just a performance group - we are storytellers for the Kingdom. Through the powerful mediums of theater, film, and creative arts, we communicate timeless biblical truths in ways that resonate with contemporary audiences.

Founded on the belief that drama can be a profound tool for evangelism and discipleship, our ministry combines artistic excellence with spiritual depth. We produce original Christian films, stage compelling plays, and create digital content that addresses real-life issues from a faith perspective.

Our vision is to raise a new generation of Christian creatives who will use their talents to influence culture, challenge perspectives, and lead people to encounter Jesus through the power of storytelling.

Our Creative Teams

Film Production

Our film team writes, directs, and produces Christian movies that address contemporary issues with biblical wisdom. From scriptwriting to post-production, we create content for cinema, television, and digital platforms.

  • Scriptwriting & Story Development
  • Cinematography & Directing
  • Editing & Post-Production
  • Sound Design & Original Scores

Theater & Stage

Our theater team produces live performances, stage plays, and dramatic presentations for church events, conferences, and community outreach. We specialize in both contemporary and classical Christian theater.

  • Stage Acting & Performance
  • Set Design & Construction
  • Costume & Makeup Design
  • Lighting & Stage Management

Digital Content

Our digital team creates short films, skits, and video content for social media and online ministry. We produce engaging content that reaches younger audiences where they are - on digital platforms.

  • Short Films & Web Series
  • Social Media Skits & Reels
  • Animation & Motion Graphics
  • Video Editing & Effects
