About SPAC Nxtion UK

Bringing the SPAC Church vision to the United Kingdom with cultural relevance and spiritual power.

Our Vision in the UK

To establish a vibrant, multicultural church that impacts the United Kingdom with the gospel, raising leaders who transform their spheres of influence.

Community Focus

We're committed to serving the Nigerian diaspora and multicultural communities across the UK, providing spiritual guidance and practical support.

Global Connection

As part of the global SPAC Church family, we maintain strong connections with our headquarters in Nigeria while contextualizing our ministry for the UK.

Our Pastor

Leading SPAC Nxtion UK with vision, passion, and apostolic grace

Pastor Tobi Adegboyega

Lead Pastor, SPAC Nxtion UK

Pastor Tobi Adegboyega is a dynamic, anointed minister of the gospel with a passion for raising the next generation of Kingdom leaders. With over 10 years of ministry experience, he brings fresh fire and apostolic insight to the United Kingdom.

Born into a family of ministers, Pastor Tobi was called into ministry at a young age. He holds a Master's degree in Theology from the University of London and has been instrumental in planting and growing churches across Europe.

"Our mandate in the UK is to raise champions who will influence every sector of society with Kingdom principles and demonstrate God's love in practical ways."
