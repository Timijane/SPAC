Our US Lead Pastor

Leading the SPAC vision across America with apostolic grace and pastoral wisdom

Pastor Dapo Adegboyega

US Lead Pastor, SPAC Church America

Pastor Dapo Adegboyega is a visionary leader with a passion for planting life-giving churches across America. With over 15 years of ministry experience spanning two continents, he brings a unique blend of African spiritual vitality and American cultural insight.

A graduate of Liberty University with a Doctorate in Ministry, Pastor Dapo has planted multiple successful churches and trained hundreds of leaders. He is married to Pastor Bimbo Adegboyega, and they are blessed with three children.

"Our mission in America is to raise a generation of believers who not only know God's Word but demonstrate His power in every sphere of society."
