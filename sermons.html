"Arise, shine, for your light has come, and the glory of the LORD rises upon you." - Isaiah 60:1

THE ARISE MOVEMENT

More than an event—it's a monthly encounter designed to awaken a generation.

Awakening a Generation to Purpose

The ARISE Conference was birthed from a burden to see young people awakened to their divine purpose and equipped with spiritual fire for the end-time harvest.

Each first Saturday of the month, we gather not just for another church program, but for a divine appointment—a time where heaven meets earth in the lives of young people.

This is where destinies are clarified, spiritual gifts are activated, and a generation arises to take its place in God's kingdom agenda.

Prophetic Atmosphere

Encounter God's voice through prophetic ministry and words of knowledge.

Fire Impartation

Receive tangible spiritual impartation for ministry and marketplace impact.

Strategic Prayer

Engage in targeted prayer sessions that break barriers and release destiny.

Kingdom Networking

Connect with like-minded young people pursuing God's purpose.

Hosted by Pastor Yomi Okewunmi

Dynamic minister with apostolic and prophetic mantle

MONTHLY GATHERING SCHEDULE

A typical ARISE Conference flow. Times may vary as we follow the Spirit's leading.

6:30 PM - Arrival & Fellowship

Welcome & Connection

Arrive early for pre-service fellowship, prayer, and connecting with other young believers.

7:00 PM - Opening Session

Worship & Praise

High-energy worship led by the ARISE worship team. Creating an atmosphere for encounter.

7:45 PM - Word & Impartation

Teaching Session

Powerful, relevant teaching from our anointed ministers. Focused on activation and application.

8:30 PM - Fire Tunnel

Ministry & Impartation Time

The highlight of the night. Personal prayer, prophetic ministry, and impartation of spiritual gifts.

9:15 PM - Altar Response

Commitment & Commissioning

Response time for salvation, healing, and dedication. Commissioning for the month ahead.

MEET OUR HOST

The visionary behind the ARISE Conference movement.

Visionary & Host

Pastor Yomi Okewunmi

A dynamic minister with years of experience in youth and young adult ministry, Pastor Yomi carries a unique apostolic and prophetic mantle for awakening a generation. His passion is to see young people encounter God in transformative ways and step into their divine assignments.

With a background in theology and practical ministry, Pastor Yomi combines deep biblical insight with relatable application for today's youth. He serves as the Youth and Young Adult Pastor at SPAC Church and travels nationally speaking at youth conferences and leadership summits.

"My assignment is to sound the alarm for this generation to ARISE from slumber and take their place in God's end-time agenda."
