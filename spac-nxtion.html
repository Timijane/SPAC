Our Pastor

Pastor Tobi Adegboyega

Lead Pastor, SPAC Nxtion UK

Pastor Tobi Adegboyega is a dynamic, anointed minister of the gospel with a passion for raising the next generation of Kingdom leaders. With over 10 years of ministry experience, he brings fresh fire and apostolic insight to the United Kingdom.

Born into a family of ministers, Pastor Tobi was called into ministry at a young age. He holds a Master's degree in Theology from the University of London and has been instrumental in planting and growing churches across Europe.

"Our mandate in the UK is to raise champions who will influence every sector of society with Kingdom principles and demonstrate God's love in practical ways."

Leadership Development Class

Equipping the next generation of leaders for Kingdom impact in the UK and beyond

Transformational Leadership Training

The SPAC Nxtion UK Leadership Development Class is a comprehensive training program designed to equip emerging leaders with the spiritual foundation, practical skills, and character development necessary for effective ministry and marketplace leadership. Our program is rooted in biblical principles and tailored for the UK context.

Program Overview

This 12-month intensive leadership program meets bi-weekly on Saturday mornings from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Each session combines teaching, practical workshops, mentorship, and hands-on ministry experience. The curriculum is divided into four quarters, each focusing on a critical aspect of leadership development.

Curriculum Structure

Quarter 1: Spiritual Foundations
• Understanding Your Calling and Purpose
• Developing a Consistent Prayer Life
• Biblical Principles of Leadership
• Character Development and Integrity
• The Role of the Holy Spirit in Leadership

Quarter 2: Ministry Skills
• Effective Communication and Preaching
• Pastoral Care and Counseling
• Team Building and Volunteer Management
• Worship Leading and Creative Arts
• Children's and Youth Ministry

Quarter 3: Organizational Leadership
• Strategic Planning and Vision Casting
• Financial Stewardship and Budgeting
• Church Administration and Operations
• Conflict Resolution and Mediation
• UK Legal Framework for Churches

Quarter 4: Practical Ministry
• Church Planting in the UK Context
• Multicultural Ministry Approaches
• Digital Ministry and Social Media
• Community Outreach and Evangelism
• Leadership Transition and Succession

Program Requirements

• Regular attendance (minimum 80% attendance required)
• Active participation in all workshops and group activities
• Completion of assigned readings and projects
• Participation in a ministry placement within SPAC Nxtion UK
• Submission of a final leadership project

Admission Criteria

Applicants must be at least 18 years old, committed members of SPAC Nxtion UK for a minimum of 6 months, demonstrate leadership potential, complete the application form including a personal essay, provide two references (one pastoral), and be available for an interview with the leadership team.

Program Benefits

• One-on-one mentorship with experienced leaders
• Access to exclusive leadership resources and materials
• Opportunity for ministry placement and leadership roles
• Networking with other emerging leaders
• Potential recommendation for further theological education

Tuition and Fees

The program tuition is £300 for the entire 12-month period, payable in quarterly installments of £75. Scholarships and payment plans are available for those with financial need. All tuition payments go toward program materials, resources, and administrative costs.

Application Process

Applications are accepted twice per year (January and August). The application window opens six weeks before the program start date. Interested candidates should complete the online application form, submit required documents, and schedule an interview. Applicants will be notified of acceptance decisions within two weeks of their interview.
